Projects & Experience

Welcome to my portfolio of projects and experiences. Here, I showcase a variety of apps and tools I've built, spanning fields like sports technology, health monitoring, and educational AI.

Gymnastics Tracking App

A cross-platform app for tracking training sessions in power tumbling or gymnastics. Athletes can log equipment usage, track session difficulty levels, and visualize progress through weekly summaries. Built with Flutter and Supabase, using Clean Architecture principles and BLoC for state management.

Sonohaler

A medical app for monitoring respiratory health. My job was end-to-end AI, consisting of filtering sound, feature extraction, creating models and implementing the models. All ML/DL was done using Tensorflow, while the models were implemented into their Flutter app.

Sonohaler website

Taptics

Duolingo for football tactics. Coaches can create puzzles and quizzes for their athletes. It is made with Flutter and Supabase. I've made the app from scratch, following design from co-founder.

Study Agent

A small passion project, making a tool for students to learn more efficient, while trying out the newest Langgraph library. It utilizes Langgraph together with SQL and Chroma, for profiling students and saving study material.

One-Click Chatbot

A prototype for creating new chatbots for a CMS. With one api call, a new group of resources is made in Azure, with everything needed for a company to get their own chatbot. Made with Azure functions, Bicep and prototyped with Streamlit.

Personal Website

A simple HTML, CSS and JS website for displaying my portfolio.

VisAlgorithms

A regression model for an energy trading client. By using APIs to gather data, the model reliably forecasted energy prices up to four hours in advance, enabling the client to make informed, real-time decisions in a complex market.

AI Konsulenterne

For this project I worked on putting together multiple different chatbots. The chatbot flow was made with Flowise, while setting up vector databases in Pinecone and saving documents in Azure. Some chatbots was also made with Azures AI Search, but this set of tools ended up being overkill.
